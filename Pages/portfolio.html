Portfolio Website

I have created a personal portfolio website with JavaScript, HTML, and CSS that presents a person's significant bio. The website is made to effectively highlight background, projects, and skills.

The purpose of this portfolio website is to serve as a central venue for online workers, freelancers, and YouTubers to present their information and demonstrate their talents. Visitors can easily discover everything there is to know about the person in no time at all by simply clicking on the detailed information that is readily available.

There are four pages

  • Home
  • Contact
  • About
  • Service

Home Page

The homepage includes basic information such as a CV, bio, and the latest project that was completed. A slider containing feedback from clients could be located at the bottom of the main page.

Service Page

You can find significant information regarding the services provided by the provider on this services page. To help new clients understand and have clarity, each service is described in great depth. This design makes it possible for users to peruse with convenience and discover the person providing the services, giving them the information they need to make intelligent choices.

Contact and About page

An About page and a Contact page are two more pages on the website. The company or individual is fully described on the About page, which also offers insights into their background and mission. A handy form to get in touch with the website owner or business may be found on the Contact page.
